What is Everstoke?

Brian from BKXC and Aaron from MTBing Adventures partnered up in 2021 to purchase 91 acres of rugged land to build mountain bike trails.

Little by little, the dream of Everstoke is being realized. We're constantly improving the property with amazing mountain bike trails AND with the essential infrastructure components to make the place livable.

We picked this location near Graeagle because of the epic mountain bike rides nearby and the constant building of new trails by the Sierra Buttes Trail Stewardship.
