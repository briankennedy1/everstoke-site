Trail Map About Us

91 acres of mountain bike paradise in The Lost Sierra

We're building a private MTB community to have a ton of fun together on and off the bike in Blairsden, California.

This region is the next big thing and you should be a part of it. We have our own purpose-built trails and we are surrounded by an endless array of public trails. It would take weeks to ride them all.

Everstoke's highest point is at 4,900 feet, which means our trails ride early in the spring and late into the winter. In the shoulder seasons the snow melts quickly, leaving hero dirt behind.

Our neighbors are the national forest and a brewery, what more could you ask for?
